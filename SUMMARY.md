#### Short names:

- MPRT: Maximum/Minimum paths to reach a target: $$dp[i]=max(dp[path1_to_i], dp[path2_to_i],...,dp[pathn_to_i])$$.
- DW: Distinct ways: $$dp[i]=dp[path1]+dp[path2]+...+dp[pathn]$$
